Achievements

Participating in the Western Access Video Excellence (WAVE) Awards is one of my greatest accomplishments. The WAVE Awards program is a competition of community media. Earlier this year, I created a documentary film with Community Media Access Collaborative (CMAC). With the help of CMAC staff my documentary, Our Disappearing Wetlands, was able to be submitted into the competition and was awarded as the top video in the Community Issues student division. I have also achieved a Coursera certificate from completing the online course, Programming with JavaScript. I learned the basic concepts of JavaScript such as working with functions, arrays, variables, data types, and much more. Completing such an engaging course was incredibly rewarding with knowledge.
